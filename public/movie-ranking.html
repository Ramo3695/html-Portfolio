The Best Movies According to Omar

My top 3 movies of all time

The Wolf of Wall Street

This is my favorite movie of all time. It is a drama that is very intense and has a great story. Leonardo DiCaprio is amazing in this movie and one of my favorite movie actor.

The Mask

This is a classic movie that I have watched many times. It is a comedy that is very funny and great to watch with family.

Training Day

This is a great movie that I have watched many times. It is a crime drama that is very intense and has a great story. Denzel Washington is amazing in this movie and one of my favorite movie actor.
